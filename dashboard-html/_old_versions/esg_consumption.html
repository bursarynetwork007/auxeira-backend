Life Below Water: Conserving Our Oceans for a Sustainable Future

Protecting marine ecosystems, fostering sustainable fisheries, and combating ocean pollution for a thriving planet.

17+ Million Metric Tons

Marine pollution clogging oceans in 2021, projected to double/triple by 2040.

37.7%

Global fishery stocks overfished in 2021.

126 Countries

Engaged in marine spatial planning (2024), but only 45 with approved plans.

Compelling Success Stories

Nicaraguan Hawksbill Project

Protected over 500 nests, released >50,000 hatchlings through community involvement and incentives.

Coral Heat-Tolerance Research

Increased heat-tolerance of algal symbionts, improving coral bleaching resilience for restoration efforts.

Fin Whale Population Recovery

Global population reached ~100,000 mature individuals; status moved from "endangered" to "vulnerable" (IUCN 2018).

Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park

High abundances of sharks due to effective management and strict no-take rules, a model for MPAs.

Living Portfolio: Companies Making a Difference

The Ocean Cleanup

Developing technologies to remove ocean plastic pollution, aiming for 90% reduction by 2040.

Oceana

Largest international advocacy organization for ocean conservation, driving policy change and public awareness.

Good Catch Foods

Produces plant-based seafood alternatives, reducing pressure on wild fish stocks.

Ocean Power Technologies (OPT)

Provides autonomous maritime solutions and wave energy conversion for cleaner ocean operations.

Marine Stewardship Council (MSC)

Global non-profit setting standards for sustainable fishing, promoting responsible practices.

Coral Vita

Coral reef restoration using microfragmentation to grow and outplant corals to degraded reefs.
