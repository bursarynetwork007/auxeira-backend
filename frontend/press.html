Company Overview

Transforming Startup Failure into Predictable Science

Auxeira is revolutionizing entrepreneurship by combining behavioral economics, AI, and blockchain technology to systematically improve startup success rates.

Our Startup Success Engine (SSE) is the world's first autonomous behavioral intervention platform specifically designed for entrepreneurs, addressing the $1 trillion annual startup failure crisis through scientific methodology.

Company Evolution: Auxeira represents a strategic pivot from LanteOTC, focusing on deeper value creation in the investment space by addressing the root causes of startup failure through behavioral science and systematic intervention.
