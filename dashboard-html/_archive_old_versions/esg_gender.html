Compelling Success Stories

The Nature Conservancy's Forest Program

Focuses on protecting forests and promoting sustainable forest management. Their work in the Amazon has protected millions of acres of rainforest, contributing significantly to climate change mitigation and biodiversity preservation.

WWF's Forest Initiatives

Addresses threats to forests globally, aiming to conserve the world's forests by 2030. Their efforts in Southeast Asia have led to the recovery of endangered species populations and critical habitat restoration.

Boeing's Pollinator Prairie

A project dedicated to restoring native habitats and supporting pollinator populations. This initiative has successfully increased pollinator species diversity and educated the public on ecological importance.

Living Portfolio: Investments in Action

The Nature Conservancy

A global non-profit focused on land and water conservation, climate change, and biodiversity protection across over 70 countries.

Google

Supports environmental monitoring and research for biodiversity through initiatives like the Google Green Program and Google Earth Engine.

Unilever

Committed to sustainable sourcing, aiming for 100% agricultural raw materials sustainably sourced by 2025, with strong supply chain transparency.

Danone

Focuses on regenerative agriculture and sustainable sourcing, utilizing a biodiversity scorecard to assess and improve its impact across the value chain.

GSK

Aims for positive biodiversity at its owned sites by 2030, implementing biodiversity action plans and investing in habitat improvement.

IKEA

Committed to its Forest Positive Plan, aiming to plant 1 billion trees by 2030, alongside water stewardship and sustainable materials programs.

New Investment Opportunities

Sustainable Forestry Funds

Investments in sustainably managed timberlands and reforestation projects that generate carbon credits and conserve biodiversity.

Biodiversity Offset Projects

Funding for projects that create or restore habitats to compensate for biodiversity loss elsewhere, often linked to development projects.

Eco-tourism and Conservation Ventures

Investments in businesses that combine sustainable tourism with direct conservation efforts, supporting local communities and wildlife protection.

Restoration Technology Startups

Companies developing innovative technologies for ecosystem restoration, such as drone-based reforestation, AI for species monitoring, or advanced soil remediation.

Comprehensive Impact Metrics

Global forest cover continues to decline, albeit at a slower rate. Sustainable forest management practices are crucial to reverse this trend.

While protected areas have increased, progress has stagnated since 2015, highlighting the need for renewed conservation efforts.

The Red List Index shows a significant deterioration in species extinction risk, emphasizing the urgency of biodiversity protection.
