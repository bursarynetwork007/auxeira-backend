Compelling Success Stories

Afo Acuaponia: Sustainable Urban Farming

Afo Acuaponia in Colombia integrates ancestral knowledge with technology to create urban farms. They produce organic fish and vegetables using 95% less water and avoiding 0.4 kg of CO2e per kg of food, promoting sustainable food production in urban areas.

Bancalimentos: Waste-to-Food Exchange

Bancalimentos in Colombia tackles food insecurity by exchanging recyclable waste for essential goods. They have engaged over 10,000 users, recycled 2,880 tons of solid waste, and distributed over 2 million food items, empowering rural communities.

Nutrivida: Fortified Food for Malnutrition

Nutrivida, a social business in Costa Rica, provides fortified food products to combat malnutrition. Since 2013, they have provided food to over 2.7 million people and donated over 650,000 food items across Central America, significantly improving nutrition.

Living Portfolio

Indigo Ag

A leading agricultural technology company focused on sustainable farming practices, improving soil health, and reducing carbon emissions through microbial treatments and digital technologies. They work with farmers to implement regenerative agriculture.

SAI Platform

A global initiative that unites over 190 food and drink companies to accelerate the adoption of sustainable agriculture practices worldwide. They develop tools and guidance for sustainable sourcing.

Paine Schwartz Partners

A private equity firm specializing in sustainable food chain investing. They invest in companies across the food and agribusiness sectors that are driving innovation and sustainability.

Heifer International (Impact Capital)

Provides affordable finance and support to smallholder farmers and cooperatives globally, helping them build sustainable livelihoods and achieve food security through livestock and agricultural development.

New Investment Opportunities

Regenerative Agriculture Funding

Investment in platforms like Steward that provide business loans and financial support to regenerative agriculture producers, enabling them to scale sustainable farming practices and improve soil health.

Food Waste Valorization Technologies

Opportunities in startups and technologies that convert food waste into valuable products, such as biofuels, animal feed, or new food ingredients, addressing both environmental concerns and food security.

AI and IoT in Precision Agriculture

Investing in AgriFoodTech startups that leverage Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things (IoT) for precision farming, optimizing resource use, improving crop yields, and enhancing supply chain efficiency to reduce hunger.

AI-Powered Insights and Recommendations

AI-Driven Crop Optimization

AI models can analyze vast datasets on soil conditions, weather patterns, and crop genetics to recommend optimal planting strategies, irrigation schedules, and pest control, significantly boosting yields and reducing resource waste.

Predictive Food Crisis Alerts

Leveraging machine learning, systems can predict potential food shortages or crises by analyzing climate data, market prices, and geopolitical factors, enabling proactive interventions and humanitarian aid deployment.

Personalized Nutrition Guidance

AI can offer tailored dietary recommendations based on individual health profiles, local food availability, and cultural preferences, helping to combat malnutrition and promote healthier eating habits.
