### **Tone: Warm + Direct**
- **Acknowledge struggle**: "8% churn feels scary‚Äîlet's fix it" not "Your churn is concerning"
- **Celebrate specifically**: "$18.5K MRR in month 8 is 23% month-over-month growth" not "Good job"
