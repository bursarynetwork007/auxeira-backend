3.4 billion people lacked safely managed sanitation services in 2024.

Compelling Success Stories

Story 1: Fit for School Programme (Southeast Asia)

The Regional Fit for School (FIT) programme supported national and local governments to develop national standards and implementing guidelines for Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH) facilities in schools. This initiative has significantly reduced preventable diseases like diarrhea and acute respiratory diseases among school children by promoting daily handwashing with soap and brushing teeth with fluoride toothpaste. The program has been rolled out in Cambodia, Indonesia, Lao PDR, and the Philippines, reaching millions of students.

Impact: Improved health and learning conditions for millions of primary and secondary school children. Reduced incidence of water-related diseases.

Story 2: Enhanced Rural Resilience in Yemen (ERRY)

In Yemen, one of the most water-poor countries, the ERRY project addressed challenges by supporting displaced and marginalized youth and women through decentralized solar energy systems. This initiative improved access to energy, employability skills, stable income, and self-confidence. Solar-powered micro-businesses were established, providing sustainable energy and income in remote areas.

Impact: Improved access to sustainable energy and water solutions, leading to economic empowerment and increased resilience for vulnerable communities in Yemen.

AI-Powered Insights and Recommendations

Insight 1: Predictive Maintenance for Water Infrastructure

AI analysis of sensor data in water distribution networks can predict equipment failures and pipeline leaks before they occur, optimizing maintenance schedules and significantly reducing water loss. This proactive approach can save up to 20% of treated water currently lost to leaks.

Insight 2: Personalized Hygiene Education Campaigns

Leveraging AI to analyze demographic and behavioral data can enable the creation of highly targeted and personalized hygiene education campaigns. This increases engagement and effectiveness, leading to better adoption of critical hygiene practices in diverse communities.

Recommendation: Invest in Climate-Resilient Water Solutions

Given increasing climate variability, AI models recommend prioritizing investments in climate-resilient water infrastructure, such as drought-resistant water sources, flood-proof treatment plants, and advanced water recycling systems, to ensure long-term water security.
